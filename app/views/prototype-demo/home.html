Dartford Crossing
(Dart Charge)

Use this service to:

  • pay for pre or post crossings
  • pay or challenge a penalty charge notice (PCN)
  • create and manage an account
  • check payment for crossings

Paying the charge

You must pay the Dartford Crossing charge by midnight the day after you cross.

You can get a fine if you're late or do not pay.

Know more about charges and fines

Other ways to pay

  • pay with cash at your nearest Payzone store
  • create an account by post
  • pay in advance or top-up your account by post
  • pay or create an account by phone

Dart Charge contact centre

  • Phone: 0300 300 0120
  • From outside the UK: +44 (0) 300 300 0120
  • Textphone: 18001 0300 300 0120
